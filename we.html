The Life of Six Cats

I have six cats. Two kittens that are my babies and are seven months old. Their names are Luna and Minerva. Luna is a pure white kitten that does not like any humans except for her mom (me). Minerva, who we call Minnie is a pure black kitten who is very playful and loves to cuddle with others. Minnie used to have a brother kitten named Severus who unfortuanatly got sick and died. One of our cats is three years old and never comes in the house but loves to sit on top of cars, her name is Ella, short for Cinderella. Cinderella is a grey cat with a white tummy and white patterns on her ears. The other three cats are foster cats, two of them are kittens and the other one is their mom who we call Feral Mommy, because she is feral so she hisses at all humans. Her to kittens however, are adorable and love human contact, their names are Anchovie and Sardine. Anchovie is a super small female kitten who is pretty timid but loves playing with her brother and sleeping on warm surfaces such as peoples legs. Sardine however is bigger than his sister and loves playing with everything and exploring everywhere he can. The three foster cats are tuxedo cats, so they have black fur with white stomachs. These are all my fur babies so come check out my crib.
